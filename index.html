Overview

Are you ready to have your brain pushed to its limits while also having loads of fun? Are you ready to get your adrenaline rushing? Well get ready to play the ULTIMATE trivia game: Race to the Crown. Unlike any other Trivia game, questions transpire from various topics, like science, geography, pop-culture, and technology. Players will then pick an avatar and race their way around the board, answering multiple questions along the way, until one reaches the crown. Additionally, there may be some alternate paths to get to the crown faster, and some stomps along the way. Therefore, getting to the crown is not an easy path, as with every wrong answer comes a consequence. Some of these consequences might even get your heart racing a bit. Throughout this game it is hopeful that players will have gained a lot of knowledge while also having a lot of fun. Are you the knowledge guru? Do you have what it takes to capture the crown? Well play and find out!!

How to play:

You will select your character and your name when you click out of the page. There is an alert system where it will update your next moves.

How to win?

Whoever gets to the crown first will be declared the grand champion.

On your turn:

  1. Roll the dice
  2. The number you roll will be counted towards the spaces you have remaining
  3. Pick a card based on the color of the space you land on
  4. A question will pop up and you will have 30 seconds to answer it
  5. If you get the question correct, remain at that space
  6. If incorrect spin the punishment wheel and do as says

Punishment Wheel

  • Move Back Two Spaces: 2 spaces will be added to the number of spaces left to the crown
  • Move Back Five Spaces: 5 spaces will be added to the number of spaces left to the crown
  • Move Back Ten Spaces: 10 spaces will be added to the number of spaces left to the crown
  • Go Back to the Start: Progress to the crown resets back to 27 spaces to the crown
  • No Punishment: you got lucky and nothing happens!
